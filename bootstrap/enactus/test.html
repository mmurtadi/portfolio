WE ARE WINDSOR!

We are a local chapter of Enactus, a non-profit organization that seeks to better the lives of others using the power of entrepreneurship.

We are a community of student, academic, and business leaders that strive to empower local members, instil entrepreneurial action, and enhance the economic health of our community by enabling the progress of our student leaders and establishing sustainable projects throughout the Windsor-Essex region.

Project Releaf

Project Releaf is a venture created to empower local villagers in Marite, South Africa by giving them access to clean drinking water through the creation and distribution of an affordable water filter.

CookStart

CookStart is a social enterprise for newcomers to Canada. The aim of this project is to develop a sustainable food business incubator that will support food entrepreneurs in the Windsor-Essex region.

Windsor SOUP

Windsor SOUP is a monthly micro-funding dinner that brings the community together. Presenters will have the opportunity to showcase their ventures and potentially ubtain microfunding.

CityThrive

CityThrive is a 12-week program that helps individuals on social assistance start or develop their own small business through one-on-one mentorship.

Youthrive

Youthrive is a 10-week program that we have integrated into various high schools throughout Windsor-Essex. We currently have 12 schools participating.
